About

Paul and the Yum-Yum Crew: The Eternal Search(PYYC:ES) is a puzzle-platforming game created during the spring semester of 2015. It was started around early March until May 13th. Team size was five. Game was made using MonoGame in Visual Studios.

The Story

The game centers around Paul, the protagonist, who lost all his memories after a tragic accident that killed him. Unfortunately, Paul is stuck between the Physical world and the Afterlife. If he wants to be at peace, he must recollect all his memories that have been scattered across the lands. Unfortunately, many different creatures and objects are blocking his way. Paul must switch between the Physical world and the Afterlife in order collect his scattered memories.
